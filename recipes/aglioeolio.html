~ Aglio e Olio ~

Description:

Perfectly cooked pasta with a beautiful sauce made from garlic, fresh parsley, and olive oil. Finished with a squeeze lemon juice and a pinch of chili flake

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Lemon Juice
  • Chili Flake

Steps:

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Heat Olive Oil
  3. Cook garlic until lightly golden
  4. Add 1/2 cup of pasta water and cook until thickened
  5. Add cooked pasta and chopped parsley
  6. Finish with lemon and chili flake
