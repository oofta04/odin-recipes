~ Smashburgers ~

Description:

2 juicy beef patties cooked and crisped to perfection and topped with american cheese!

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • American Cheese

Steps:

  1. Toast burger buns
  2. Heat skillet with oil
  3. Season ground beef with salt and pepper
  4. Add beef to hot pan and smash with spatula
  5. Once Beef is cooked through, add cheese until melted
  6. Put between toasted buns and enjoy!
